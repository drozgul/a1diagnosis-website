# A1 Diagnosis — Market Opportunity & Financial Projections

## Company Overview
- **Product**: First blood-based AMD (Age-related Macular Degeneration) risk assessment test
- **Accuracy**: 94-95% test accuracy rate
- **Price**: $250 per test (B2B invoice to clinic)
- **Model**: B2B laboratory services (CLIA LDT pathway)
- **Launch Target**: Q1 2027

## Unit Economics

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Revenue per test | $250 |
| Variable COGS | $60/test |
| **Contribution Margin** | **$190/test (76%)** |
| Monthly Fixed Costs | $34,700/month |
| **Breakeven** | **~183 tests/month (~9 tests/business day)** |

### Variable COGS Breakdown (per test)

| Cost Component | Amount |
|----------------|--------|
| Reagents & consumables (solvents, buffers, LC column wear, calibrants) | $26 |
| Sample collection kit (blood tubes, labels, packaging) | $7 |
| Shipping - cold chain (temperature-controlled transport) | $23 |
| AI/ML compute (cloud processing per sample) | $3 |
| Report generation (risk report creation & delivery) | $1 |
| **Total Variable COGS** | **$60** |

**Note**: Native peptide advantage — skipping the trypsin digestion step saves $10-15/test vs traditional proteomics.

### Monthly Fixed Costs

| Category | Amount | Details |
|----------|--------|---------|
| Production Fixed (COGS) | $23,200/mo | LC-MS/MS lease $15K + CLS tech $3.2K + QC $5K |
| SG&A Fixed Costs | $11,500/mo | Lab director $5K + Lab space $4K + LIS/IT $2.5K |
| **Total Fixed** | **$34,700/mo** | |

### Gross Margin Calculation (at 500 tests/month)
- Fixed production COGS per test: $23,200 ÷ 500 = $46/test
- Gross Margin: $250 - $60 - $46 = **$144/test (58%)**
- SG&A sits below the gross margin line

### Margin Benchmarks
- Exact Sciences (at $2.76B revenue): **69% gross margin**
- Guardant Health (at $622M revenue): **62% gross margin**
- A1 Diagnosis target at scale: **65-70% gross margin**

## B2B Service Flow

1. **Eye Doctor Orders** → AMD risk test
2. **Blood Draw at Clinic** → Simple collection
3. **A1 CLIA Lab Analyzes** → AI/ML risk scoring
4. **Risk Report to Doctor** → Actionable results
5. **Clinic Pays A1 $250** → B2B invoice

### Money Flow
- **A1 Diagnosis**: Invoices clinic directly ($250/test lab services model)
- **Eye Doctor / Clinic**: Bills insurance, keeps reimbursement margin (revenue-positive service)
- **Patient**: Gets test at routine eye visit (no extra visit needed)

## Market Sizing Methodology Note

Bottom-up analysis using U.S. Census Bureau demographic data, CMS healthcare engagement statistics, AMD risk factor prevalence studies (NEI, AAO), and insurance coverage rates. SAM reflects serviceable market accounting for insurance coverage, healthcare access, and proactive health engagement patterns.

**Disclaimer**: Unit economics represent management estimates based on comparable CLIA LDT laboratory operations. Variable COGS and fixed costs are projections subject to vendor contracts, volume discounts, and operational scale. Growth projections based on conservative clinic partnership assumptions. References to Exact Sciences, GRAIL, and Guardant Health are based on publicly available information and SEC filings.
