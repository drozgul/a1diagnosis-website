👁 The diagnostic gap in ophthalmology: 25% of AMD cases are missed during dilated eye exams (Neely et al., JAMA Ophthalmology 2017) — both ophthalmologists and optometrists miss at equal rates. Among missed cases, 30% had large drusen consistent with intermediate AMD. A1 Diagnosis provides a complementary blood-based risk stratification tool that helps eye care providers catch what imaging alone misses and prioritize high-risk patients for closer monitoring.
